Seeds Guidance

PaddySeeds

Paddy seeds are filled to 3/4 level in each of 2-4 drums, and once the seeder is pulled, seeds fall in 4-8 rows @ 20 cm width between the rows paddy is the major crop cultivated in more than 22 lakh hectares during Kharif and Rabi seasons

Coriander Seeds

Coriander seeds are used in cooking to flavor meat, fish, and vegetable dishes. They can also be used to enhance salads, pickles, preserves, and alcohol. Coriander has numerous powerful antioxidants

Jatropha Seeds

The Jatropha seed contains 46.27% oil.Even the seed cake produced as a by-product is used as an excellent organic fertilizer.Jatropha crude oil was obtained by pressed the dried seeds using a machine and the crude oil used for biodiesel production.

Sesame Seeds

proper space plants can receive sufficient sun light, water and other necessary nutrients from the soil which enhance the yield of the sesame. Ngala et al. 2013 reported that 75 cm row spacing produced the higher seed yield as compared with 25 cm and 50 cm row spacing.

Pumpkin seeds

removing all female flowers (these have a small swelling at the base of the bloom) for the first 3 weeks.June- July and December- January are suitable seasons. Soak the seeds in double the quantity of water for 30 minutes and incubate for 6 days.

Cotton Seeds

Plant cotton seeds in a place with loose, rich soil where the plants will get at least 4 or 5 hours of direct sunlight every day. It can be grown in a pot, but the pot needs to be at least 36 inches deep. Before you plant, it helps to put an inch of compost into the soil..

RedGram Seeds

Red gram seed should be own behind the plough or with the help of seed drill at a row spacing of 60-75 cm keeping 15-20 cm distance from the plant to plant. A seed rate of 12 to 15 kg per hectare is sufficient.

GreenGram Seeds

Before sowing, seeds should be treated with Thiram or Captan @ 2 to 3 g/kg of seeds.Sowing is done on onset of the monsoon in kharif season and during second fortnight of February to first fortnight of March in summer season.

Sunflower Seeds

Rinse sunflower seeds before laying out to dry. The best time to plant SunflowerSunflower seeds contain vitamin E, flavonoids, and other plant compounds that can reduce inflammation. is from May to June as it is a warm season crop
